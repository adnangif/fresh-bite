Frequently Asked Questions

Freshbite is a food delivery platform that takes orders from customers, picks food from restaurants, and delivers it to them via a rider.

You can place an order on Freshbite through our website or mobile app by selecting your desired restaurant and menu items, then completing the checkout process.

Freshbite accepts various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and mobile wallets.

Yes, Freshbite allows you to schedule orders for a later time or date as per your convenience.

Once your order is placed, you can track its status in real-time through our app or website.

If you receive an incorrect order, please contact our customer service immediately through the app or website for assistance and resolution.

Orders can be canceled within a certain time frame after placing them. Check the cancellation policy on our app or website for more details.

You can update your delivery address in the profile section of our app or website before placing an order.

Yes, delivery fees may apply based on the distance and restaurant. You can view the delivery fee during the checkout process.

You can apply a promo code at the checkout page before completing your order. Enter the code in the designated field and the discount will be applied.

If your order is late, you can track the rider's location in real-time. If there's a significant delay, please contact our customer service for assistance.

Yes, you can tip the rider through the app or in cash upon delivery. The option to tip is available during checkout and after delivery.

Yes, Freshbite offers a variety of vegetarian and vegan options from different restaurants. You can filter these options while browsing the menu.

To become a Freshbite rider, visit our website and fill out the rider application form. Once your application is reviewed, we will contact you for the next steps.

You can report a problem with your order through the app or website under the 'Help' section. Provide details of the issue and our support team will assist you.

Yes, you can easily reorder your favorite meals from your order history in the app or website.

Yes, Freshbite offers contactless delivery options. You can select this option during checkout and the rider will leave your order at your doorstep.

You can give feedback about your experience through the app or website after your order is delivered. Your feedback helps us improve our services.

You can check if Freshbite is available in your area by entering your address on our website or app.

You can contact Freshbite customer service through the 'Contact Us' section on our app or website. We are available to assist you with any inquiries or issues.

To apply for a refund, please contact our customer service team with details of your order and the reason for the refund request. We will process your request as soon as possible.

Once an order is placed, changes or cancellations may not be possible. However, you can contact our customer service team immediately to see if any adjustments can be made.

There are generally no restrictions on the size of orders. However, large orders may require additional time for preparation and delivery.

We ensure that food is packaged securely and hygienically for delivery to maintain its quality and freshness during transit.

If your order is missing items, please contact our customer service team immediately with details of the missing items. We will investigate and arrange for the missing items to be delivered or issue a refund.

Yes, Freshbite offers catering services for events. Please contact our customer service team for more information and to discuss your requirements.

Currently, orders can only be placed from one restaurant at a time. If you wish to order from multiple restaurants, you will need to place separate orders.

Some restaurants may have minimum order requirements. You can view any such requirements on the restaurant's menu page.

You will receive a confirmation email or notification on the app once your order has been successfully placed and confirmed by the restaurant.
